Annual Car Show

Bring your antique, classic, or modern car, pick-up truck or motorcycle to join over 100 others at the car show on Saturday, June 7, held from 8:00 a.m. - 3:00 p.m. The judged event awards twenty-five Top 25 and specialty trophies and $2,500 for the Top 4 overall awards.
