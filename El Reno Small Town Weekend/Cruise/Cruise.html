Car cruise

Saturday wraps up with another favorite: a closed cruise ($25 pre-paid). Line up your vehicle at 6:00 p.m. and join hundreds of others in a leisurely, four-mile cruise between 7:00 – 9:00 p.m. on Route 66 through El Reno. Registration is from 8:00 a.m. – 9:00 p. m. and vehicles need to be 1999 and older.
