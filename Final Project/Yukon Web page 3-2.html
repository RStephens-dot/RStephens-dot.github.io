Facts:

  • Yukon sits between Oklahoma City and El Reno on Route 66.
  • The town has 25,500 residents and its four largest employment types are health care, school systems, retail and construction
  • The community hosts many festivals to celebrate its heritage, such as Rock the Route, the Oklahoma Czech Festival, and the Chisholm Trail Festival.
