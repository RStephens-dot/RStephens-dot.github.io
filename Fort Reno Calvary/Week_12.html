Fort Reno Calvary Competition

The National Cavalry Competition was held at Fort Reno, Oklahoma, from September 11-14, 2024. The U.S. Cavalry Association holds it every year.

A total of 10 contests, such as Military and Combat Horsemanship, Mounted Saber, Mounted Pistol and Military Field Jumping, engaged 50 military and civilian participants.
